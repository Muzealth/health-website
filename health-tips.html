5 Tips for a Healthy Life

  • Eat healthy: Include fruits and vegetables in your meals.
  • Exercise regularly: At least 30 minutes of physical activity every day.
  • Drink enough water: Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Get enough sleep: Aim for 7-8 hours each night.
  • Take breaks: Rest and relax to reduce stress.
